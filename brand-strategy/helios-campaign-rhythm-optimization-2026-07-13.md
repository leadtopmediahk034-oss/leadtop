# Helios Campaign Rhythm optimization

## Scope

- Give Campaign Rhythm a full desktop viewport so the annual planning system reads as a primary operating layer.
- Keep tablet and mobile layouts content-driven rather than forcing viewport height.
- Replace repeated generic Helios thumbnails with quarter-specific creative and landing-page examples.

## Case material

- Q1: spring skincare launch and seasonal landing page.
- Q2: Mother's Day beauty gifting and campaign landing page.
- Q3: back-to-school / Prime Day product mix and promotional landing page.
- Q4: Black Friday / Christmas gifting and promotional landing page.

The eight case crops are derived from the existing Helios Campaign Rhythm reference board and stored under `public/helios/campaign-cases/`.
