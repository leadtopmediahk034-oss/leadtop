# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Domestic Chinese B2B export companies, manufacturers, industrial suppliers, and outbound business owners evaluating overseas growth partners. They are usually comparing agencies, asking whether their website can generate real inquiries, and looking for a partner that understands both traffic acquisition and independent-site conversion.

## Product Purpose

Leadtop presents Polaris Growth System as a B2B independent-site inquiry growth solution. The page should turn a cold domestic visitor into a qualified consultation lead by making the offer clear, credible, and easier to evaluate than broad media-agent or generic website-building competitors.

## Brand Personality

Measured, expert, growth-oriented. The voice should feel senior and practical: confident without exaggeration, polished without becoming vague, and specific about execution.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing pages, decorative gradient blobs, oversized icon-card grids, vague "one-stop marketing" slogans, and resource-boasting pages that do not explain execution. Avoid looking like a cheap template website builder or a pure advertising agency.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the business outcome: high-quality overseas inquiries, not service inventory.
- Show the system: connect traffic, conversion, trust, and data instead of listing isolated tactics.
- Make expertise scannable: use diagrams, tables, and structured copy so decision-makers can evaluate quickly.
- Earn trust with restraint: mature spacing, readable typography, concrete process, and proof placeholders.
- Keep conversion close: repeat diagnostic CTAs at natural decision points without making the page feel aggressive.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, readable Chinese typography, visible focus states, reduced-motion support, and mobile-first responsive layouts. Avoid relying on color alone to communicate system categories.
